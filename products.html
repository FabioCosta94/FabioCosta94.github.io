Let's talk about my Overseas experiences

I had the chance to travel a lot, and many of my travels turned out to be an adventure that shaped my soul in some way. One time, for example, I risked getting stuck in Albania because there had been problems with the ship I had to get in to get back home, and nobody could speak English to notify me of such problem. Another time I got stuck in Lion on my way to Manchester for a couple of days because of some airport bureaucracy.

Of course, I could not talk about my travel experiences without mentioning my Erasmus+ experiences I had thanks to the Assonur association. I had the chance to work with people from all around Europe (and bonded with many of them). Thanks to such, I had the chance to know some of the cultures coming from Romania, Hungary, Albania, Estonia, Serbia, and a part of Switzerland. Outside of the Erasmus experiences I also traveled to Britain, France, and Spain for entertainment purposes. I wish to travel and have many new experiences, trying to understand and live the life of the communities all around the world and make them part of me.

Let's talk about my Other experiences

In July 2020 I finished my first IT programming course "Junior Full Stack Developer" by The Net Value. The course gave me a new perspective and made me decide to grow as a developer, and maybe specialize in some programming fields, once I get more accustomed to some of them. Each year I participate in the Cagliari Global Game Jam, working for 48 hours straight to complete a project with a team created on the spot. I would recommend such experience to anyone, even to someone not interested in programming. I am open and eager to live new experiences, hoping the future will give me the chance to find more.
